Responsive Design

Mobile Column Stacking

Columns automatically switch to fill mobile screens and stack on top of each other, despite their .col-md fraction.

// Shrink your browser window to see the columns stack

.col-md-1-3

.col-md-1-3

.col-md-1-3

Responsive Design

Columns can be also given .col-sm and .col-xs fractions to make them still appear as fractions on mobile.

// Shrink your browser window to see the columns remain columns

.col-md-1-3 .col-sm-1-3 .col-xs-1-3

.col-md-1-3 .col-sm-1-3 .col-xs-1-3

.col-md-1-3 .col-sm-1-3 .col-xs-1-3

Responsive Show or Hide

You can make whole divs, etc. appear or disappear based on the screen size easily. Adding .visible-xs makes content disappear on phones, while .visible-md makes content only appear on medium sized displays.

// Shrink your browser window to see watch columns appear and disappear

.col-md-1-6 .visible-md

.col-md-1-3 .hidden-sm

.col-md-1-4 .visible-xs

.col-md-1-7 .visible-sm
